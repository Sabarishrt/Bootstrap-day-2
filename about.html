About BIT

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology is an Autonomous, Self-financing Engineering College, Approved by AICTE, New Delhi and Affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Nestled on the banks of the River Bhavani, the BIT campus provides an environment for learning in harmony with nature.

The Founder: S V Balasubramaniam (Chairman)

These words perfectly describe our aim at Bannari Amman Institute of Technology. Beyond providing a sound education, we wish to provide our students with a holistic learning experience for life. Our aim is to teach students to LEARN, not just to STUDY. Our interactions with universities around the world have taught us to look at education as a complete experience, not just academics. We realize that students spend the prime years of their lives with us, and thus the responsibility is on us to provide them with a world-class education as well as a rich campus life.

As we stand on the threshold of yet another era in human development, we are ready and eager to welcome this new Knowledge Era, which will see the supremacy of brainpower over physical and financial strengths, and total specialization over singular specialization as the path to prosperity. In such an environment, society looks to educational institutions and universities to create these critical resources.

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology aims to answer this need by providing quality education, enabling young aspirants to take on an increasingly competitive world. All academic programmes offered by the Institute are designed to foster intellectual growth and development.

The latest curriculum and insights into engineering, technology, and research, coupled with state-of-the-art infrastructure, ensure that students gain optimal advantage in advanced technical knowledge and skills.

Exploring this website will reveal more about the Institute, its philosophy of Learning, and its unparalleled educational standards. I welcome you to BIT and all that it has to offer. Come, enlive the BIT experience!
