Hostel Overview

Well-furnished hostels for gents and ladies with four, double and single occupancy rooms are available with separate guest rooms. Dining halls with a seating capacity of 6060 students, modern kitchens, cold storage, and dishwashing systems ensure comfort. The Student Centre is certified under the 5S Workplace Management System.

Gents Hostel

Men's hostel has six blocks named Sapphire, Emerald, Ruby, Diamond, Coral and Pearl. Overall, the six blocks can accommodate 3727 members in 1173 rooms.

Ladies Hostel

Women's hostel consists of seven blocks including Ganga, Yamuna, Cauvery, and Bhavani blocks. The hostels accommodate 2171 members across 636 rooms with various room configurations.
